Lean UX

Small, testable and measurable objectives. Reducing means risking little and taking the best route. Empathy and use of shared metrics allow you to follow a strategy that returns objective results.

Agile

People and ability to change. Communication and involvement of the whole team in the planning stages to meet everyone's needs and keep the process aligned.

Data driven

Base your choices on qualitative and quantitative data, not on personal opinions and beliefs. Too often top-down choices are made which at the first opportunity turn out to be bankrupt.

Facilitation

Use co-design tools and techniques to oil and improve processes. The use of structured ideation and modeling activities allows a high involvement and alignment between people.

Team UX of one
