Eidoo

Eidoo is a Swiss company that deals with cryptocurrencies, in particular its main product is the wallet of the same name. Thanks to Eidoo it is possible to receive, exchange and buy cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, EDO (the ICO of Eidoo) and many others.

Discovery and personas

We approached the project by mapping together with the client an eventstorming and some proto-personas, within an initial discovery workshop. Then, through interviews with users, we validated and consolidated the 3 main Personas by distributing them within a user journey map.

See/do maps, userflows and wireframes

Starting from the flow described by the user journey map, we created the wireframes of a first version of the application which was validated and developed in the form of a UI, and subsequently implemented. From a first iteration of what the application's MVP was, we began to integrate new features, passing from a UX analysis that included a collection of requirements that was formalized with see / do map, userflow and wiframe lo-fi that allowed rapid user testing and validation by the business.

UI and design system

The design of the user interfaces was followed in a symbiotic manner with the front-end development, working with a modular and atomic logic as possible. The formalization of some guidelines within a small design system have been very useful for creating coherence within the various Eidoo microservices.
