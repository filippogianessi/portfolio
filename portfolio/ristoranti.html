Ristoranti.it

UX DESIGN
MyPersonalGym

Ristoranti.it is a company that helps restaurateurs by providing a management service for orders, reservations, and deliveries for one or more restaurants.

The Challenge

As a UX team, we were involved to analyze the current flow of the service and its interface, to understand its criticalities and possible improvements in usability and look & feel.

Discovery

Through a discovery, we explored the domain of the project. With an elevator pitch we understood the vision of the project and the perspective of the competitors, we defined the dimensions on which to structure the first personas and finally we mapped the flow through an eventstorming, identifying actors and pain points.

Heuristic Analysis and UI Inventory

As a first analysis, we took a photograph of the current interface by creating a UI inventory and a heuristic analysis. These have identified further critical issues compared to those highlighted in the discovery workshop, thus allowing us to create a backlog prioritized by the stakeholders of interventions on the user experience of the applications.

Interaction design

We started the user research with interviews: we interviewed 5/7 people for each proto-persona. We asked them specific questions to validate the assumption made into the discovery, but also explorative questions about training habits, indoor training experiences, mindfulness, and life balance.
